Research

Research Interests

AI-driven bioinformatics, multi-omics integration, causal inference, precision medicine, cancer, asthma, and allergic diseases.

TRACE Framework

Development of TRACE (Trans-mQTL Atlas of Causal Epigenetic Regulation) framework integrating SNP→CpG→TF→Gene regulatory networks with multi-omics validation.

Goals

Translate CpG-based causal discoveries into clinically actionable biomarkers, advancing precision medicine across diseases.
